About Me - Abhiram Reddy

Hello, I'm Abhiram Reddy, and I am the founder of Nagireddy Organization. The organization is named in honor of my father. I have a passion for riding bikes and a strong desire to serve people in need. Having faced challenging situations myself, I understand the importance of support. I contribute whatever is possible for me and encourage others to join the cause. Through Nagireddy Organization, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of those facing hardships.

Our Services

Food Donation

"We distribute nutritious meals to individuals and families facing hunger, as well as to those who are homeless, helpless, and staying beside the road, extending our support to the most vulnerable members of our community.".

Clothing Assistance
